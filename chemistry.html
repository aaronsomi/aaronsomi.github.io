About me

Hi I'm Saoirse! I am currently in my second year of a Joint Honours in Biology and Psychology at Durham University and am available throughout the week to teach Biology at GCSE and A Level. About me

I am currently in my third year of a Dual Bachelor’s Degree in Mathematics and Physics at Durham University. I am available throughout the week to teach Mathematics and Physics at levels ranging from GCSE to A-Level and International Baccalaureate (SL/HL). I am an experienced tutor and, having taught GCSE resit students at Varndean College for a year, would like to help students understand the topics they are struggling with.

I am open to different teaching methods to best suit the student.
