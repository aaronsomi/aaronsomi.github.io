Prices

Each tutor will state their hourly rate on their subject profile but rates should range between £25-£35 per hour session. Each session will have been prepared for a minimum of one hour before the lesson to ensure that students are given the highest quality learning experience available.

Payment Protection

The site owners process the payments for each session and so if you are not fully satisfied with your first session with a particular tutor we will be happy to discuss refunds.
