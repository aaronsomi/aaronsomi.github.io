About me

I am currently studying for a PhD in Mathematical Physics. I am available throughout the week to teach Mathematics and Physics at levels ranging from GCSE to A-Level and International Baccalaureate (SL/HL). I am an experienced tutor and focus on teaching students why particular ideas in Mathematics and Physics are important, rather than focusing on algorithmic thinking. Whether you are looking to study Engineering, Physics, Mathematics or other STEM related subjects, I hope to provide insight into the deeper and more fundamental ideas occuring in Mathematics and Physics.

The sessions will begin with an introduction to the desired topic, followed by a development of intuition around the subject and finally some problems to solve together. I am open to different teaching methods to best suit the student.

Prices

I currently charge £40 per hour session for IB/A-level and £30 per hour for GCSE. The sessions will be done using Bitpaper, an online tutoring interface. Video calling is optional depending on preference of the student.

Experience

I am a PhD student in Mathematics with a class-I degree in Mathematics and Physics from Durham University. I have over 5 years of tutoring experience ranging from teaching GCSE resit students at Varndean College for a year, tutoring with Durham University in the Levelling-up programme (A-level) and I have also provided private tuition to a variety of students from 11+ to IB level.
